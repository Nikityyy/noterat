Our Code

Built on Alpine Values

Four principles forged from the philosophy of Digital Alpinism.

Comradery

Kameradschaft

We are in this together. The app is a partner, not a judge. We celebrate the wins and stay calm during the losses.

Precision

Präzision

Reduction

Install Noterat on iPhone

Add the web app to your home screen in four steps. It works just like a native app.

1

Open in Safari

Open the Noterat web app in Safari. This only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the share icon (square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari.

3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen."

4

Tap "Add"

Confirm the name and tap Add. Noterat will appear on your home screen as a standalone app.
